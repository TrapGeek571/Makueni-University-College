How to Apply

Applications are open for Government-sponsored (KUCCPS) and Self-sponsored students. Ensure you meet the minimum requirements for your chosen school.

  • Step 1: Select your preferred program from our Schools.
  • Step 2: Download the application form or apply via the portal.
  • Step 3: Attach certified copies of your academic certificates.
  • Step 4: Submit to the Registrar (Academic Affairs).
